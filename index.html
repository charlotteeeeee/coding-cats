Are you GreenCurious?

What we can do Why? Current Affairs

GreenCurious is a collective for people who want to learn more about the environment, and what choices they can make that can better the world around them! The choices you make today will change the world of tomorrow!

This site contains resources you may find useful on your 'Green Journey' - your path towards leading a greener life! Here you will be able to find our top tips, as well as where to find out more information from reliable sources, such as GreenPeace and Department for Envrionment,Food and Rural Affairs

There are many ways you can get envolved. Every change counts. What will yours be?

Who we are:

GreenCurious was established in 2018 by the CodingCats group: Allie Dakin, Beth Reyniers, Charlotte Ekins and Mina Oba. The website was designed as part of their CodeFirst:Girls project. They are all passionate about the environment, nature and animals (particularly cats).
